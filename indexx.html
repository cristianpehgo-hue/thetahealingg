Actualmente imparto clases y terapias tanto en línea como presenciales, atendiendo a clientes en diversos países de Latinoamérica, Asia y Europa.

¿Qué es ThetaHealing®?

ThetaHealing® is a world-renowned meditation technique and spiritual philosophy founded by Vianna Stibal that has been transforming lives in more than 180 countries for over 30 years.

Through a focused meditative state, you learn to connect with the Creator of All That Is and identify the beliefs held within the subconscious mind. As limiting beliefs are released and replaced, greater clarity, peace, balance, and purpose can be experienced in everyday life.

From this connection comes insight, healing, inner growth, and the ability to create meaningful change naturally. ThetaHealing® is designed to empower you mentally, emotionally, spiritually, and energetically—helping you awaken to your highest potential and live with greater joy.
